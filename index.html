    About

    Living with dementia is a debilitating disease that can be disruptive to an individual, affectiving many aspects of their life. Optimistic gives you the tools to stay ahead of dementia and live a healthy life.

    An all-in-one place that connects your tasks, reminders, and support group to aid you in your everyday life.

    Dementia is a collection of symptoms that can affect people in a variety of ways. These symptoms can vary from each individual, but often include memory loss, difficulty concentrating and being able to carry out everyday tasks. They may also easily become confused about their surroundings and what they are doing at a given time

    Our application brings several important tools together in one easy to use location to aid individuals and their family members in the care and support of someone with dementia

    • - A way for friends and family to connect on the app to help set reminders
    • - Help individual suffering from dementia stay on top of their daily to-do list
    • - User can easily reach anyone in their support group or contact for help if they don't feel safe.

    Call for Help

    If you are in need of help or support, click the "Call for Help" button and you will be routed to the Alzheimer's Association 24/7 Helpline where someone can assist you. Their team is available around the clock, 365 days a year. Call toll-free at no cost to you.
